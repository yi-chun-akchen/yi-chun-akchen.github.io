Yi-Chun Akchen (formerly Yi-Chun Chen)

I am an Assistant Professor at the UCL School of Management, University College London, since August 2023. My research aims to understand customer choice from a data-driven perspective and provides operational insights using machine learning and optimization techniques.

I received my PhD from the UCLA Anderson School of Management, where I was fortunate to be advised by Professor Velibor Mišić. I received my master’s degree from Stanford University and bachelor’s degree from National Taiwan University.
